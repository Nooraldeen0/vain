Give up

In the end, I wasn't the one who picked the roses. I guess Looking from afar is now enough for me, I was a little ink splattered on a paper when the writer was writing a story. It was a limitless slope, pain after pain. It's like loss of appetite but towards the world.

In the middle of the night, it gets so silent, the sky gets clear, no birds whistling, and no people outside, it's when I remember I didn't progress, I feel helpless, weak, and sad, It's the only time loneliness invades my heart, I feel shattered inside.

No words can explain nor feelings can flow, no tears can fall nor mind can stop thinking, then I start thinking of giving up, and the void starts eating me alive.

As soon as all of this fades, then I hold my book and start learning, In such time the only ting can help is learning, for someone who replaced thier friends with books, but I believe One Day, I will meet who understands me.
